About The Game

Garden Survivors is an addictive survival game where you must protect your precious bamboo garden from endless waves of monsters. Collect power-ups, upgrade your character, and survive as long as possible in this challenging adventure.

As time progresses, enemies become stronger and more numerous. Strategize your movements and attacks to maximize your survival time and achieve the highest score possible!

The game features intuitive controls, stunning visuals, and an engaging progression system that will keep you coming back for more. Can you become the ultimate Garden Survivor?

Game Features

Fast-Paced Action

Experience thrilling gameplay with non-stop action as you face waves of unique enemies.

Character Progression

Upgrade your character with powerful abilities and items as you progress through the game.

Strategic Gameplay

Plan your moves carefully to outlast the increasingly difficult waves of enemies.

How To Play

  1. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the garden
  2. Automatically attack nearby enemies
  3. Collect experience gems from defeated enemies to level up
  4. Choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones when you level up
  5. Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves
